About

Mobile and web developer with over seven years' experience I have supervised developers and managed projects. I have moved major projects to iterative development cycles focused on high priority features with code reviews and improved unit testing and code quality. This has improved customer satisfaction due to better communication and reduced defects and made for happier developers too.

Innovation I love to tackle the most difficult problems and I excel at delivering functionality quickly while balancing the quality and maintainability of the code.

Growth I seek out opportunities to foster growth and positive change in myself, my projects, my teams and my organizations.

Community My passion extends outside of my job to my work in the community as an active member of the local tech scene as an organizer and teacher.
